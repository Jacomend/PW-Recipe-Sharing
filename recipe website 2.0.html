The Ken Special

Ingredients:

  • 1 hot dog bun

  • 8 oz Canned Tuna

Instructions:

Cook the brat and add tuna to the assembled hot dog and enjoy!

The Emmons

Ingredients:

  • 1 Kosher dog
  • 1 hot bun
  • 1 oz of cream cheese
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard

Instructions:

Heat the kosher dog. Toast the bun. Spread cream cheese on bun. Cut up Jalapeno into 1/8 inch slices.Put jalapeno slices on hot dog. Then put mustard on hot dog
